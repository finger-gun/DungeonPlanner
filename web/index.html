Why DungeonPlanner?

Tile-Based Building

Snap rooms together on a grid. Walls, doors, stairs — everything clicks into place. Build a full dungeon in minutes.

Real-Time 3D

See your dungeon come alive in full 3D with dynamic lighting, atmospheric fog, and smooth camera controls.

Multi-Floor Dungeons

Stack floors above and below. Cellars, towers, catacombs — your vertical designs stay connected and navigable.

Props & Furnishing

Place torches, chests, barrels, altars and more. Each asset snaps to walls or floors exactly where it belongs.

Save & Share

Export your dungeon as a portable JSON file. Load it back anytime, share with your party, or build a library of maps.
